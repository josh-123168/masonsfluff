Steps to achieve Mason's Fluff:

  1. Get in the Shower for 15 min fluffing your hair. No shampoo! Make sure your hair is extra greasy.
  2. Dry hair with a towel.
  3. Blow-dry hair for 10 minutes.
  4. Fluff up all sides of your hair evenly.
  5. Put 10 shakes of hair texture powder in your hair. Spread evenly for max fluffage!
  6. Finally, apply 30 sprays of Sea Salt Spray.
